A semi-regular meetup for London JavaScript developers

There are no talks or set agendas. It's simply a place to chat, where everyone just happens to be a JavaScript developer.

The next GinJS will be on Thursday November 7th. Follow @ginjs_london on Twitter for the ticket announcement.

Rules

  • You must be a person who can make things in JS - i.e., a developer or designer. People learning are welcome too.
  • Be civilised.
