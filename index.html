A meetup for London JavaScript developers and designers

It's kind of like the fun bit after an event, without anything beforehand. There are no talks or set agendas. It's simply a meet other JavaScript people and talk about what they're working on or whatever else they feel like.

The next GinJS is at 7:30PM on Thursday January 9th at the The Book Club in Shoreditch.

Then follow @ginjs_london on Twitter for updates, and so we know who you are!

Date and Location

That's 7:30PM on Thursday January 9th at the The Book Club in Shoreditch.

Nearest tube is Shoreditch High Street or Old Street.

Rules

  • You must be a person who can make things in JS - i.e., a developer or designer. People learning are welcome too.
  • Be civilised.
